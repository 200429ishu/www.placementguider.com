HINDUSTAN COMPUTERS LIMITED

HCL stands for Hindustan Computers Limited. It is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) service and consulting company headquartered in Noida, India. It was founded in 1976 as one of the original IT start-ups in India. It is a pioneer of modern computing with many inventions including the introductions of the 8-bit microprocessor-based computer in 1978. HCL has more than 131000 employees from 140 nationalities operating across 44 countries, including over 500 branches in India. The enterprise generates annual revenues of more than US$ 8.3 billion. Today, HCL enterprise is offering services in different sectors, which include technology, healthcare, and talent management solutions. Accordingly, it comprises three companies -
  • HCL Infosystems
  • HCL Technologies
  • HCL Healthcare
What does HCL company do?
The company offers a range of solutions in IT infrastructure management services, digital process operations, cloud native services, cybersecurity services, digital and analytics services, DRYiCE, IoT works, HCL software services, SIAM/XaaS products & advanced services, industry software services,etc.,
